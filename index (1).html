Muay Thai In America

Muay Thai or the sport of Eight limbs, is a martial art that first originated in Thai Land in the 16th century. It was condsidered a peace-time martial art amongst the soilders of King Naresuan. The sport itself was evolved for the purpose of hand-to-hand combat and used as a weapon in war. Eight limbs goes as follows: shins, knees, elbows, and fist. The first set of rules for Muay Thai were created in the 1600's according to Thai history and it wasn't until the 1930's that the rules of Muay Thai we're fianlly offically recorded.

The Science of Eight Limbs

The Science of Eight Limbs is considered to be a very deadly art. It not only involves tremendous physcial skill, but also a great level of intelligence.As I mentioned above, Eight Limbs consist of the following: shins,knees, elbows, and fist. All these bodily weapons are used to execute technique and skill within the ring when fighting. Scoring may vary in many instances depending on certain countries and or states. Kicks can score the highest within a fight under most saunctioning bodies. Other cases knees. Some judges may be more enthusied by the skillful boxing or agression that is on display. Its important to understand and know your rule set as per where you are fighting. Having a seasoned coach and teammates is key to a successful Muay Thai career.
