Heavy Hitters

There are some amazing and talented Thai fighter tha are still active in this current generation. In Thai Land children start their journey in Muay Thai around the ages of 5 or 6. Fighting in Thailand is a way of life for the people there. They earn their currency in Bhat, which is the currency of Thailand. On avergae, fighters can make around 10,000 to 15,000 baht per fight, which is about $300 - $450 USD. Top tier fighters can earn up to 60,000 baht, or around $4,000 USD. Fighters of lower rank make even less, around $100 per fight. With this money they are able to support their families. Saenchai

Suphachai Saepong, also known professionally as Saenchai, was born July 30, 1980. Saenchai is a professional Muay Thai fighter and former professional boxer. He is also a former six-time champion across four across four divisions at Lumpinee Boxing Stadium.
